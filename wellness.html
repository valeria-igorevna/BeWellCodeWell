Well-being is especially important for coders, because programming work is intense, mentally demanding and stationary, which puts a strain on both body and mind

wellbeing

Regular breaks are crucial in maintaining physical well-being. Small breaks, where the coder gets up, stretches and moves, can prevent the disadvantages of sitting.

breaks and stretches

One of the most important ways to prevent mental strain is adequate rest. Lack of sleep weakens concentration, memory and decision-making ability, which directly affects work quality and efficiency

Rest

Remote working has become more common among coders, which can bring its own challenges to social well-being. In a remote work environment, it is important to keep in active contact with colleagues, for example through virtual meetings or instant messaging.
